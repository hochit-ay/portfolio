Introduction

續杯 (Cantonese: Zuk6 Bui1), a tea leaf brand hailing from the vibrant streets of Hong Kong, encapsulates the essence of tradition and innovation in every cup. Embracing the dynamism of Hong Kong's cosmopolitan spirit, Zuk Bui infuses its teas with a touch of modernity, offering a unique sensory journey that transcends time and tradition.

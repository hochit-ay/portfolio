About

Dorothy Au-Yeung

Dorothy is a versatile graphic designer with extensive experience in the field, a passion for drawing and a background in fine art.
She has also honed her skills as a bespoke artist, specializing in hand-painting techniques.
Her artistic journey has led her to work with renowned luxury brands, where her expertise in crafting has been highly valued.
Based in both Hong Kong and Ottawa, Dorothy brings a unique blend of cultural influences to her work.
